HOW TO GET THERE?

Via Commute:
Bus: Ride the bus with routes going to Balayan, Nasugbu, or Calatagan that can be found in Buendia and Cubao terminals. From Metro Manila going to Balayan, Batangas, expect 1.5-2.5 hours travel time and don’t forget to advise the bus driver or the bus driver’s staff to drop you at Waltermart Balayan. From there, you can ride a tricycle to RCD Royal Homes Balayan. It's as simple as that, and you'll arrive at your destination.

Via Private Vehicle:
When driving your own vehicle to RCD Royale Homes Balayan, Batangas, you have several route options. You may take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) and exit either at Carmona, Cavite or Sta. Rosa, Laguna. Continue straight, following the signs, and you will reach RCD Royale Homes Balayan. For easier navigation, you can pin “RCD Royale Homes Balayan” in Waze or Google Maps.
