About This Property:

Sprawling over 3 hectares of land, located in the city center of the province of Cavite, RCD Villas De Trece is a community that offers us over 440 units of townhouses. RCD Land aims to give the chance to the ordinary citizens of the Philippines to own a home through this affordable housing without giving them a high price tag.

Villas De Trece offers our homeowners to experience a suburban living but still accessible to the daily essentials of its people. Trece Martires is bounded on the North by the city of Gen. Trias where lots of businesses and office hubs are waiting for its people. An addition to this is that it can provide its residents the close proximity to establishments such as SM Trece Martires, the City Hall, the Public Market, and some of the best schools in Cavite, it really provides you a quick access to what you need.

The RCD Villas De Trece offers modern-designed townhouses and simple rowhouses that are priced P600,000 to P1.3 million, perfectly designed for middle to low-segment earners. Live your dream home and live the suburban lifestyle here in RCD Villas De Trece!

HOW TO GET THERE?

Via Commute:

From Coastal Mall:
Ride a bus going to Dasma or Tagaytay. Then asked the driver to drop you at Robinson’s Dasma, then ride a jeep going to SM Trece and advise the driver to drop you off at SM Trece. From SM Trece you can call a friendly tricycle driver that will help you get to RCD Villas De Trece.

Via Private Vehicle:
Use the road going to Coastal then take an exit going to Bacoor, go straight in Aguinaldo Highway then turn right at Robinsons Dasma’s intersection, go straight until you reach SM Trece and after 1 km ahead from SM Trece turn right, in 500 meters you will reach RCD Villas De Trece’s site.
