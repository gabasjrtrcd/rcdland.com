Property Description

Strategically located in the progressing city of San Jose del Monte in Bulacan, RCD Winterbreeze Homes Bulacan is an affordable socialized housing project with 788 units sprawling on 5 hectares of land. Specifically designed and intended for minimum-wage earners, you can own your dream home here for just P450,000. The community is well-planned with a basketball court and a guarded entrance gate, which offers both leisure and security for your family. A 5 to 10-minute ride is all it takes to go to the MRT Line 7 San Jose del Monte Station, which makes going to the city faster and more convenient. With a balanced lifestyle of urban and rural living, RCD Winterbreeze Homes Bulacan is definitely the practical choice for you and your family.
