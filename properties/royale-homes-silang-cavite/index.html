Property Description

Live your suburban dreams in RCD Royale Homes Silang! With 1,066 housing units standing on 21 hectares of land, RCD Royale Homes Silang is a well-planned community that is complete with amenities such as a guarded entrance gate, a basketball court, a swimming pool, and a clubhouse. It is strategically located in a highly urbanized residential area which is accessible to schools, public markets, hospitals, churches and shopping malls. Getting to and from the Metro is hassle-free with public transportation that is easily accessible few minutes from E. Aguinaldo Highway, the subdivision is placed along East-West Road and a 4-km short drive from Cavite-Laguna Expressway (CALAX). The cold breeze can be felt with Tagaytay being 10 minutes away from the property. Set within a scenic and relaxing environment, this home is definitely your breath of fresh air from the city. For as low as P1.7 million, you can live the serene lifestyle you’ve always wanted here in RCD Royale Homes Silang!

HOW TO GET THERE?

Via Commute:
Ride a van going to Robinson’s Dasmariñas. Then from Robinson’s Dasma, ride a jeep or bus going to Silang and Tagaytay. Advise the driver that your drop off is at Premiere Plaza, from Premiere Plaza you can call a friendly tricycle driver that will help you get to RCD Royale Homes Silang, Cavite. From Coastal Mall Ride a bus going to Tagaytay or Batangas. Tell the bus diver to drop you at Premiere Plaza, from Premiere Plaza you can ask a friendly tricycle.

Via Private Vehicle:

Via SLEX
From Starmall Alabang, drive along SLEX and turn right to Carmona Exit, go straight, and when you reach SM Dasma, turn left going to Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway nearest landmark is Robinsons Premiere Plaza.

Via Daang Hari Road
Drive along Daang Hari road, go straight until you reach District Imus mall and turn left going Tagaytay via Aguinaldo Highway.
