Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

RCD Land, Inc. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website www.rcdland.com (“Site”), including any forms, property listings, and broker accreditation features.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide via inquiry forms, broker accreditation, or newsletter sign-up.
  • Property Preferences: Details about properties you view, save, or inquire about.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and usage data collected via cookies and analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide property information.
  • To process broker accreditation and related applications.
  • To improve our website, services, and user experience.
  • To send you updates, newsletters, or promotional materials (if you opt-in).
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We may share your information with trusted service providers (e.g., email, hosting, analytics) who assist us in operating the Site.
  • We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal interests.

Cookies & Tracking

Our Site uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze usage. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
  • You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.
