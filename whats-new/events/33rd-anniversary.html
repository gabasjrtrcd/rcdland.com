RCD Group Celebrates 33rd
Anniversary by Being One with
Our Frontliners:

by Shane Bugay, published on November 18, 2020

  • Author:
  • Category: Anniversary
  • Date: August 18, 2020

The month of August has always been the most-awaited time of the year for RCD Group. This year, the company decided to not only support the housing needs of the Filipinos but it extended its hands to our frontliners and healthcare workers during this period of time by partnering with one of the most trustworthy charity in the Philippines known with the organization’s name, Operation Blessing.


RCD Group’s 33rd anniversary was held last August 16, Sunday, on a Zoom party/meeting. To wear red was an instruction to everyone who will join the zoom anniversary party, to commend the sacrifices of our fronliners during the fight with Covid-19. The main goal was to let the employees feel their importance and their purpose even everyone can be miles away. It was indeed a celebration of fun with a purpose.


The company prepared different raffle prizes to everyone who participated the anniversary celebration. Almost a hundred of employees get to enjoy the prizes that they won that was draw by an electronic raffle. Prizes includes: 40 winners of 300 GCASH, 20 winners of 500 GCASH, 10 winners of 1000 GCASH, 5 winners of UVC Sterilized Wand with Contactless Key, 2 winners of UVC Lamp Home Use, 1 winner of Air Purifier, 1 winner of 3000 GCASH, and a winner of 5000 GCASH.

It is really indeed the best time to make an investment for a home, aside from being an imperishable asset it is always ever increasing in value. It is just like what RCD Land, Inc. is, a company that grows through the years by building and providing the Filipino a quality and affordable home. The company always believes that every Filipino has the right to own a home even up to the low-segment earners. RCD’s purpose is to always serve the needs of the Filipino people and to contribute in solving the issue of the growing housing backlog in the Philippines.

As President and CEO, Mr. Roland Delantar, explained in his speech, that to keep going during this time in the whole world is already an achievement for everyone who has been part of RCD. He always reminded us that RCD group was built because of the hard work and individual contributions of every employees. RCD Land, Inc. may be affected by the pandemic but it is surely fighting for the company and for its people, it will never stop serving and building every Filipinos dreams.


Through its 33 years of experience in the industry, RCD Land, Inc. has never stop evolving and putting up its communities in Silang, Bacoor, General Trias, Paranaque, San Jose Del Monte, Norzagaray, Tuy, Trece Martires and Mariveles.

The company dedicated its success to the professional management team and to the people who always made it possible for RCD Land, Inc. to stay on the line with its mission – to be a beacon of hope for every Filipino to realize their dream of owning a home. Carrying RCD’s three main undertakings: Realizing dreams, Creating Opportunities, and Delivering commitments.

There may be few things in our life which have had less effect on us, but as what RCD Land, Inc. aims during it’s 33rd anniversary you can have your home and at the same time you can help the world to heal and fight as one.
