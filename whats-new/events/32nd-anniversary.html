RCD Group dedicates its 32nd
Anniversary to its people

RCD Group continues to soar and aim high in the real estate industry as it celebrates its 32nd anniversary last August 16, 2019.

  • Author:
  • Category: Anniversary
  • Date: August 16, 2019

With the success of its mission to improve lives by providing quality and affordable homes to every Filipino, RCD Group dedicates its triumph to its pool of passionate workers and sellers.

RCD Group’s 32nd anniversary was all about its people. The anniversary celebration was held in R&R Resort in Biñan, Laguna last Friday, August 16. A relaxed picnic party was the theme of the event, the main goal was for the employees and members to enjoy, bond, and have fun. It was definitely a day free of stress and hassle, a breath of fresh air from the usual work scenario for everyone!

Six employees were recognized for their continuous support and loyalty to the company. To show appreciation to these people, a 14K gold ring was awarded to each.


Mr. Joselito Bermiso, Ms. Shiela Caguicla, and Ms. Lettie Finn Relosa have been loyal to the company for 5 years.


On the other hand, Ms. Maryknoll Vilarba and Ms. Rhoda Velunta have been with the company for 10 years..


And of course, Mr. Raul Castelo has been recognized for his 15-year loyalty and support to RCD Land, Inc.


In connection to RCD Group’s anniversary, a sellers’ awarding was held in Romalaine Seafood Restaurant & Leisure Park in Mariveles, Bataan last Sunday, August 18. This is to recognize the top performing sales agents, directors, and brokers that have been continuously supporting RCD Land’s projects.


A trip to Cebu and Bohol with P10,000 pocket money was awarded to Aline Aguinaldo, Jovi Dhoneal Lepasana, and Susana Ador for being our annual salesperson awardees.


Moreover, an International trip to South Korea with P35,000 pocket money was awarded to Elmira Mae Delos Santos.


Of course, a trip to Japan with P50,000 pocket money was awarded to Merlene Cariño for being our annual sales director awardee.


Last but not the least, 110-sqm lot in Silang, Cavite, and P100,000 cash incentives were awarded to MMD Realty Marketing Services Ltd. Co. for being our annual awardee for the broker’s category.


Like a garden filled with strong trees, the real estate industry has been filled with bigger companies equipped with deeper sources of funding and commercial advantages of name-recall. RCD Land was just a plain and simple company that is rooted to dedication and passion to improve the lives of Filipinos. With supportive people and team members who religiously watered this simple root with hardwork and determination, RCD Land flourished and blossomed in the industry.


As President and CEO, Mr. Roland Delantar, expressed in his speech, “Just to survive this long in the business is already a tribute to our resiliency, our collective heart to stay in the race and to actually win our share of victories while competing in an industry dominated by corporate giants. We already made our point; we delivered our message loud and clear. Thirty-two years is more than enough proof. RCD Land is as much the product of our collective hard work as it is the outcome of your priceless individual contributions.”


With housing as the company’s flagship business, RCD Land, Inc. has been credited for putting up thriving communities in Silang, Bacoor, General Trias, Parañaque, San Jose Del Monte, Norzagaray, and Tuy. Moreover, additional communities are being built in the town of Mariveles, Bataan and Trece Martires, Cavite.

The company’s success is deeply rooted to the people who made it possible. RCD Land, Inc. stays true to its mission—to improve the lives of every Filipino by building quality and affordable homes. With the fulfilment of our clients’ needs is also the fulfilment of our people’s dreams. Indeed, time has taught us an important lesson that it doesn’t matter how big or small the role that we play is, it is how we play our role that ultimately makes the difference.

Thus, expect RCD Land, Inc., with the help of its people, to play its role in the industry with the passion to create and the heart to succeed for every Filipino. With RCD Land, we are improving lives.
