RCD Royale Homes Tuy holds a
Blessing Ceremony for its
Subdivision Amenities

RCD Royale Homes just held a blessing ceremony for its subdivision amenities last Sunday, December 1, 2019, at Brgy. Sabang, Tuy, Batangas.

  • Author: Nicole Dizon
  • Category: Anniversary
  • Date: August 21, 2021

With the completion of the subdivision’s amenities, the Blessing Ceremony commemorated RCD Royale Homes’ continuous progress and development. The event was attended by RCD Management, staff, marketing partners, and the subdivision homeowners.


A holy mass was led by Fr. Albert Guiritan, embodying the Catholic practice that signifies the protection of the inhabitants from unforeseen and unexpected circumstances. Subsequent to the mass, the blessing and ribbon-cutting of the Basketball Court, Swimming Pool, and Clubhouse transpired. Apart from these religious practices, throwing of candies and coins were also done to symbolize the unceasing prosperity and blessings within the subdivision, the people, and the whole company.


Roland Delantar, RCD Land’s President and CEO, stated that, “We can already see the huge progress here in RCD Royale Homes Tuy—the completion of the subdivision amenities, the continuous construction of single-attached houses in Phase 1, the progressing land development in Phase 2, and the increase in the constructed townhouse units in Phase 3. We are enthusiastic to see what lies ahead and how we can further improve lives through our projects.”


Mr. Delantar also encouraged everyone to visit the fully-constructed turnover units in Phase 3. High ceilings, spacious living area, and well-ventilated units—these townhouses exemplify the quality yet indeed affordable homes that RCD promises to deliver to its stakeholders.

The town of Tuy is an emerging economic and residential zone with its various infrastructures and developments that are conducive to its economic growth. RCD Royale Homes Tuy is conveniently located in Tagaytay-Nasugbu Highway and is just a few minutes away from the upcoming Cavite-Tagaytay-Batangas Expressway (CTBEX), allowing its stakeholders to live in close proximity to the Metro.

Provision of amenities in the subdivision ensures the homeowners’ leisure and relaxation within the premises of their own, upholding RCD Land’s mission to improve the lives of the Filipinos by providing quality and affordable homes that also enables the ideal lifestyle.


As a gift this Christmas Season, RCD Royale Homes Tuy released a NO DOWNPAYMENT scheme until December 31 for their Phase 3, which makes the townhouse units more attainable for everyone. With just P10,000 reservation fee, buying your dream home is made even more affordable and achievable with RCD Royale Homes Tuy!
