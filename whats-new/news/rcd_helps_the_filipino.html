RCD, Helps the Filipino to Fight this Pandemic by Donating Hundreds of Sacks of Rice

RCD Land Inc. partnered with Operation Blessing Foundation Philippines Inc. the humanitarian arm of CBN Asia to extend help and reach out to more people in need beyond its organization. by Shane Bugay, published on November 20, 2020

Author:Marion Shane Bugay    Category: Anniversary   Date: August 16, 2019

This 16th of August 2020, RCD Land launched a relevant advocacy promo in celebration of their 33rd anniversary. The event was held via Zoom with almost 500 participants.

Mae Delantar, Vice President of Sales and Marketing, spearheaded the event announcement that RCD Land will be donating one thousand pesos (Php 1,000) for every reservation on any RCD Land Projects all throughout the anniversary campaign. She encouraged everyone to support the Anniversary promo to continuously help our kakabayans in guiding them in choosing the right investment, assisting them in finding quality and affordable homes, while simultaneously becoming a channel of blessing during these challenging period.

Agents, brokers, in-house sellers, homebuyers, and all stakeholders can take part in this advocacy movement. It is an embodiment of RCD Land’s efforts to enable a platform wherein everyone can show and give support during this time of crisis. Fulfilling our dreams is already something we all want to achieve, but instigating change and assistance by fulfilling your dreams is what RCD Land advocates for.

Last November 2020, Representatives of RCD Land formally awarded the said donation to Operation Blessings held in their Head Office in Operation Blessing Foundation Phils Inc. 5th Floor Sagittarius Bldg., HV De La Costa Salcedo Village, Makati City. RCD Land garnered fifty-one thousand pesos (P51,000) from all unit reservations from duration of the anniversary campaign. Rooted to the company’s mission to continuously improve lives, this step is RCD Group’s response to the Covid-19 outbreak which aims to help the Filipino people to rise as one.

This is just a small help compared to the gravity of the situation, but the aim for this is to create ripples of hope to the Filipino people during these uncertain times. RCD Land enables a platform to extend help which shows how it stays committed to its promise to continuously improve lives, deeply rooted to serve and help people further than its business and organizational goals.
