Giving in the Midst of Pandemic: RCD Land, Inc x Operation Blessing Foundation Philippines Inc.

RCD Land Inc. partnered with Operation Blessing Foundation Philippines Inc. the humanitarian arm of CBN Asia to extend help and reach out to more people in need beyond its organization. by Shane Bugay, published on November 20, 2020

Author:    Category: Anniversary   Date: August 18, 2020

Last August 16, 2020, RCD land celebrated its 33rd anniversary via Online Zoom and launched a relevant anniversary promo that both home buyers and property sellers can participate in. Miss Mae Delantar, VP for Sales & Marketing proudly announced to the 450 participants that RCD land will be donating an amount of P1,000.00 for every reservation of RCD Land projects the entire anniversary campaign. She encouraged all RCD Land sales agents, brokers & in-house sellers to support the Anniversary promo to continuously help our kakabayans in providing them quality and affordable homes, guiding them in choosing the right investment, assisting them in finding a home and at the same time, become a channel of blessings to many people in dire of need.

Recently, Representatives of RCD Land formally awarded the said donation to Operation Blessings represented by (Name od OB Staffs) held in OB Head Office in Operation Blessing Foundation Phils Inc. 5th Floor Sagittarius Bldg., HV De La Costa Salcedo Village, Makati City. This is how RCD Group is responding to the Covid-19 Outbreak. RCD Land management felt the moral duty to help others and transform lives, a sentiment very much rooted in the organization’s mission of “improving lives”.

This is just a small help compared to the gravity of the situation, but we hope that we were able to send help and provide ease from a difficult situation to many people despite our own limitations.
