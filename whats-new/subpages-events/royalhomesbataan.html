RCD Royale Homes launches in
Mariveles, Bataan

RCD Land, Inc. launched its newest project, RCD Royale Homes Bataan, last Saturday (May 25) at Brgy. Mountain View, Mariveles, Bataan.

  • Author: Nicole Dizon
  • Category: Realstate
  • Date: May 30, 2019

The event celebrated the grand launching and groundbreaking of RCD Royale Homes Bataan. VIP officials, staff of RCD Group, brokers and agents from various real estate brokerage companies, and residents of Bataan attended the gathering.


Putting the celebration to a start, Fr. Albert Guiritan led the holy mass and the blessing of the property. This was then followed by the ceremonial dig, capsule laying ceremony, and the sellers’ commitment.


To campaign the newly-launched subdivision, a motorcade was held from Brgy. Mountain View to Sisiman Road passing along the areas of the Freeport Area of Bataan and the Municipal Hall of Mariveles.


Fulfilling its promise to improve the lives of Filipinos by providing quality and affordable homes, RCD Land continues to develop housing communities in locations outside Metro Manila that remain accessible to the city. Bataan continues to grow in population with the continuous developments in the province, thus giving rise to the demand for affordable housing for its people.


Roland Delantar, President and CEO of RCD Land, Inc., stated that, “Bataan is a place of continuous progress and growth, various developments are happening in the area such as the extension of the Freeport Area of Bataan (FAB), the structure of Mariveles’s Hall of Justice, and the construction of the 20-meter four-lane barangay road connecting to the Freeport Area of Bataan, which is actually conveniently-placed just in front of our property. With this, we are excited to be part of the development and launch RCD Royale Homes Bataan which aims to provide quality and affordable homes to the people.”


Sprawling over 10 hectares of land with plain terrain, RCD Royale Homes Bataan is a gated subdivision which offers quality and affordable homes in a peaceful and tranquil community. Strategically located just along the threshold of Mariveles, the subdivision is easily accessible from the six-lane Roman Superhighway which provides quick access to your needs.


The community is well-planned, complete with amenities such as a guarded entrance gate, a basketball court, a swimming pool, and a clubhouse. Modern-designed townhouses and single-attached homes are expected to rise in the area, all reasonably-priced and intended for the middle-segment earners.
